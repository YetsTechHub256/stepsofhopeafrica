About Us

Steps of Hope Africa (SOHA) is a charity organization based in Uganda. Our mission is to rebuild hope and create sustainable development opportunities for vulnerable communities in Uganda.

Our primary focus areas include education, healthcare, and economic empowerment. With our dedicated team, strategic partnerships, and community-driven approach, we aim to make a significant positive impact on the lives of the underprivileged.
